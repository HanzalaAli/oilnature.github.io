About Us

Tibasiima Process Engineering (TibaPEng) is a full-service engineering firm providing performance driven solutions based on the EPCm delivery model throughout the asset lifecycle from concept to decommissioning across the whole petroleum value chain and global process industries that set the stage for execution excellence. The first of its kind in Uganda, we offer a diverse range of serviceswhich include concept development, engineering design, consulting, EPC / turnkey, construction management, commissioning, operations and maintenance. Our engineering capability is complete with success driven by our rapidly growing multi-faceted team of agile professional engineers (process, piping, mechanical, civil, instrument), consultants, project managers and construction workforce embracing solutions that promote constructability and safety in the earliest phases of projects to ensure on-time and on-budget project delivery for dynamic process facilities with a business object, ‘’To Engineer Beyond Infinity’’

Engineering & Design

No project is too small or large, from a review of a single drawing to multi- million-dollar engineering efforts requiring large teams, we ensure we deliver quality to our clients. Our engineering & design services cover all specialist disciplines to cover the full project lifecycle, including conceptual designs, feasibility, pre-FEED, FEED, detailed engineering and technical support during execution. We help our clients set up the right design iterated through the best models that will be built to their satisfaction. They dream it, we design it to be built.

Operations & Maintenance

We understand the complex issues of managing assets thus we know how to push the envelope on your asset’s performance, reliability and lifetime to increase return on our client’s investment. We support oil, gas and chemical operations for upstream, midstream and downstream infrastructure, encompassing the entire asset lifecycle. Retrofits, upgrades, debottlenecking and enhancement projects can be executed smoothly, either on the entire facility, individual sections or specific equipment.

EPC / Turnkey

Our mode of service delivery allows us offer virtually any size project as EPC/Turnkey across a vast range of geographical locations. And on a local level, we have a network of domestic, smaller partners, developed over many years, providing on the ground logistical support. Our EPC/Turnkey offering includes all the various form of commonly accepted contract types, such as Lump Sum Turnkey (LSTK), Engineering Procurement Installation & Commissioning (EPIC), and Engineering Procurement Construction and Commission (EPCC)
